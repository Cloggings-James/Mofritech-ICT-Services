About Us

We are web designers and developers, software and mobile application developers based in Nairobi, Kenya. We are experts in developing custom websites and software that are meticulously tailored to meet each individual client's project needs.

At Mofritech our niche is developing websites and software with maximum impact and functionality. To guarantee that the results appropriately suit the demands of the client, we make sure we understand each project's needs and requirements.

With over 8 years of experience, we have developed websites for clients drawn from various backgrounds including but not limited to consultancy, non-profit organizations (NGOs), churches, academic institutions, e-commerce, tours and travel agencies and real estate companies. Every website designed by the Mofritech team comes with a customized content management system (CMS), making it possible for clients without technical IT knowledge to easily add and update content on their websites without needing to hire an IT expert every time there is an update to the website.

Our software development experts have over the time amassed firsthand experience in building software systems for church process automation, billing, invoicing & automatic payment collection systems, parcel delivery management systems, human resource (HR) & payroll management systems, booking systems, inventory management systems, POS (point of sale) among others. This has equipped us with an all-round technical knowhow of undertaking software development projects of any size.
